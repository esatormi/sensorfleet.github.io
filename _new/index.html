Sensor Solution

SensorFleet enables lightweight and distributed monitoring of networks close to locations where the events of interest are produced. This is accomplished with an open platform that enables equipping of sensors with different capabilities (instruments) depending on network and objectives of monitoring.

Open Ecosystem

In security nothing is set in stone. New vulnerabilities and threats emerge and require rapid response. No single security solution or vendor can cover them all. Emerging issues need quick reactions and innovative assessment and detection tools.

Embrace the ecosystem

SensorFleet has been designed as an open platform for 3rd party instruments. Utilize most suitable solutions, both from open source domain and commercial vendors.

Bring your own instruments

Sometimes instruments you need aren't available from the market. SensorFleet enables customers to run their own instruments.

Improve reaction time

Emerging threats call for new detection or scanning capabilities. With an open platform, end users are not vendor locked for new instruments.
